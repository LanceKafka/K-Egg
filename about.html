ABOUT K-EGG

Bringing the taste of Korean street food to Maysan, Valenzuela

OUR STORY

K-Egg was born from a passion for Korean street food and a desire to make it accessible to everyone in our community. What started as a small food stall has grown into a beloved fast-food establishment that serves hundreds of happy customers daily.

We specialize in Korean-style egg sandwiches, crispy corndogs, flavorful wings, and hearty rice bowls - all made fresh to order with quality ingredients. Our mission is simple: provide delicious, affordable food that brings joy to your day.
